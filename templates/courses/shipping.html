Shipping & Delivery Policy

Information about digital delivery, certificate issuance, and physical merchandise shipping timelines.

1. Digital Services — Instant Delivery

EduTech AI is a digital-first platform. All core services — course imports, AI study buddy access, focus room features, and certificate generation — are delivered instantly upon subscription activation or account registration.

There are no physical shipping delays for digital products. Your access begins the moment your payment is confirmed.

2. Certificate Delivery

Completion certificates are generated immediately after passing a final exam with a score of 70% or higher. Certificates are:

  • Available for instant download as PDF from your dashboard
  • Stored permanently in your account for lifetime access
  • Shareable via a unique verification URL

3. Physical Merchandise (if applicable)

If EduTech AI offers physical merchandise (branded notebooks, stickers, etc.) in the future, the following timelines apply:

  • Processing: 1–3 business days after order confirmation
  • Domestic Shipping (India): 5–10 business days via standard courier
  • International Shipping: 10–21 business days depending on destination
  • Tracking: A tracking number will be emailed once the order is dispatched

4. Subscription Activation

Paid subscriptions (Pro and Ultra plans) are activated automatically within 60 seconds of successful payment processing via Razorpay. If your subscription does not activate:

  • Check your email for a payment confirmation receipt
  • Refresh your dashboard — the plan badge should update immediately
  • Contact support@edutechai.com with your payment ID for manual activation (resolved within 2 hours)

5. Shipping Costs

Digital products: Free — no shipping costs. Physical merchandise (if offered): Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination and package weight.

6. Contact

For shipping-related inquiries, reach out to support@edutechai.com. Response time is typically under 4 hours during business days.
