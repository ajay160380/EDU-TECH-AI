Data Protection

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026. We respect your learning privacy and process personal data with total security.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal data necessary to establish and operate your distraction-free learning profile:

  • Account Credentials: Username, email address, and encrypted passwords.
  • Workspace Progress: Imported courses, playlists, study session logs, completed lectures, and quiz results.
  • Interactive Notes: Personal Markdown notes and custom study briefs saved within the AI tutor workspace.

2. How We Utilize Your Data

Your dynamic learning profile and user information are strictly utilized to:

  • Deliver real-time progress calculations and GitHub-style learning targets.
  • Process secure Firebase connections and SSO integrations.
  • Power context-rich summaries and conceptual study questions using external AI APIs (like Groq LLM).
  • Handle secure billing and upgrade subscriptions (Simulated Payments via Razorpay).

3. Data Retention & Integrity

We retain your notes, courses, and study targets for as long as your account remains active. You possess complete rights to modify, download, or permanently delete your user profile and all associated data at any moment directly via your Profile Settings.

4. Third-Party Integrations & APIs

To maintain our high-end educational tools, we securely exchange necessary metadata with verified third-party services:

  • YouTube API: Used solely to fetch public syllabus and timings (we never collect your personal YouTube account credentials).
  • Groq LLM / OpenAI: Utilized to parse lecture scripts and compile summaries (no personal identifiers are sent to AI servers).
  • Razorpay Checkout: Processes subscription upgrades under strict PCI-DSS security frameworks.

5. Security Measures

We leverage advanced standard encryption, HTTPS protocols, and sandboxed database systems to protect all user details from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

6. Privacy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy as our learning features evolve. We will post notification of changes immediately on this screen.
